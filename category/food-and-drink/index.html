What should you do about halal meat standards when visiting someone else's home?

If you follow a stricter halal meat opinion, you may politely ask about your host's standards without offending them. Participate in the gathering and eat vegetarian if needed. Stunning is makruh but does not make the animal haram.

Yasir Qadhi • April 22, 2025

Is vanilla extract permissible in food despite containing alcohol?

Vanilla extract in food is permissible. No food product flavored with vanilla can cause intoxication, so it does not fall under the prophetic prohibition. The same applies to nutmeg and similar extracts.

Yasir Qadhi • April 22, 2025

What is the most important advice for young Muslims in their 20s and 30s?

Young Muslims should focus on three domains: religiously, judge yourself rather than others; regarding family, cherish your parents and be patient with the cycle of life; professionally, start investing early in halal mutual funds and a Roth IRA and build passive income. The choice of spouse is the second most consequential decision after accepting Islam.

Yasir Qadhi • December 6, 2025

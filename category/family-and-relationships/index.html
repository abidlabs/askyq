What are my Islamic duties toward parents who were abusive?

The obligation of dutifulness to parents (birr al-walidayn) is diminished proportionally to the injustice they committed. A child suffering panic attacks from visiting an abusive parent is not required to do so. Some duties remain (e.g. financial help sent indirectly), but the obligation shrinks with the severity of the abuse.

Yasir Qadhi • February 26, 2020

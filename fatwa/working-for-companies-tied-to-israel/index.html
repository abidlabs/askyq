Is it haram to work for a company that has business ties to Israel?

Yasir Qadhi • September 6, 2025 • Watch on YouTube
boycottBDSIsraelPalestineGaza

Quick Answer

Yasir Qadhi differentiates between companies directly aiding and abetting genocide — such as those manufacturing weapons or building factories on occupied land — and generic corporations that merely do some business with Israel. Working for companies directly involved in weapons manufacturing or occupation infrastructure is haram and one should resign. However, the vast majority of corporations (70-80%) have some indirect ties, and declaring all such employment haram is unrealistic and not supported by the Shariah. Boycotting should be done with knowledge, following official BDS lists, not emotional reactions.

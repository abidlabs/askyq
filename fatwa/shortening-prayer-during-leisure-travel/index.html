Can you shorten the prayer (qasr) when traveling for pleasure or vacation?

Yasir Qadhi • December 2, 2025 • Watch on YouTube
salahprayerqasrtravelvacationsafarqasr vacationtraveler prayer holidayshortening prayer rulescombining prayers travel

Quick Answer

By unanimous scholarly consensus, any permissible (mubah) travel qualifies for shortening prayer. No scholar has ever restricted qasr to business travel. Vacation trips qualify.

Full Lecture Transcript (Cleaned)

The Question — 33:27

Can the shortening of the salah be done on traveling that is not for business — i.e., for pleasure and vacationing? If I take a vacation, can I shorten the salah?

The Ruling — 33:41

By unanimous consensus of all the scholars of Islam, the safar that you do that is mubah — you may shorten your salah. No scholar said only business travel qualifies. Any permissible safar — so even if you're going on a family vacation, you may do qasr of the salah. No problem.

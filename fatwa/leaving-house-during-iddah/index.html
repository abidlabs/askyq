Can a wife leave the house and go to her parents during the iddah period after talaq?

Yasir Qadhi • September 6, 2025 • Watch on YouTube
iddahtalaqdivorce in Islamwaiting period after divorceleaving house during iddah

Quick Answer

After a husband gives one talaq, the wife should remain in the same house for the three-month iddah period (three menstrual cycles) as a safeguard Allah has placed to protect the marriage. Leaving the house is strongly discouraged and removes the checks and balances designed to give the couple a chance to reconcile. However, living under the same roof is not a legal condition for the validity of the talaq — the divorce will still take effect after three cycles regardless. The exception is cases of abuse or danger, where leaving is fully permissible.

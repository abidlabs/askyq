How can divorce be finalized if a spouse has left and there is no contact?

Yasir Qadhi • August 10, 2021 • Watch on YouTube
divorce absent spouse islamtalaq without presencewife left country divorcefaskh abandonment marriageiddah after talaq

Quick Answer

The husband may pronounce talaq without the wife being present, during a permissible time, and inform two witnesses; iddah begins from that pronouncement. If a husband has abandoned his wife with no care or contact, she may seek annulment (faskh) through an Islamic court or qualified scholars, with a shorter waiting period than three divorces.

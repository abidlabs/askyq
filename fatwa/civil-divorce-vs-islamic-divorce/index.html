Does a civil divorce filed by the wife count as an Islamic divorce?

Yasir Qadhi • September 6, 2025 • Watch on YouTube
civil divorceIslamic divorcetalaqkhulfaskh

Quick Answer

When a husband files for civil divorce and signs the paperwork, it counts as an Islamic talaq because writing a divorce is the same as verbalizing it. However, when a wife files for civil divorce, the nikah is not automatically dissolved — she must take additional steps. She should either get the husband to agree (making it a khul'), or go to an arbitration committee or knowledgeable imam who can validate her case. If the husband is being unjust, abusive, or has abandoned her, the arbitration committee can issue a faskh (annulment). A wife is not a prisoner in Islam and can ultimately obtain her freedom.

Halloween should be avoided due to lingering associations with demons and the supernatural, but it does not constitute shirk or kufr. Muslims who participate out of ignorance should be treated with empathy.

Are personal private celebrations like birthdays, graduations, and promotions permissible in Islam?

Personal, private, non-religious celebrations are fully permissible. The Sharia is silent about them, and making something annual (like birthdays) does not make it bid'ah.

Quick Answer

Yasir Qadhi does not give a blanket halal/haram verdict; he urges consulting local scholars grounded in both fiqh and real-world policy. Tackling genuine violent crime is praised in principle, but in practice many agencies have surveilled and entrapped Muslims unjustly. Are Muslim community festivals and suhoor fests permissible in Islam?

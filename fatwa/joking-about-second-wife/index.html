Is it appropriate for a husband to joke about taking a second wife?

Yasir Qadhi • September 6, 2025 • Watch on YouTube

Quick Answer

The Prophet (peace be upon him) never joked about taking a second wife, and doing so is not from the Sunnah. A woman has feelings, and it is not gentlemanly for a husband to provoke those feelings by joking about or dangling the prospect of a second wife. While polygamy itself may be permissible under appropriate circumstances, threatening, teasing, or treating it as humor causes genuine hurt and is unbecoming of a Muslim husband.

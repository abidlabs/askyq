How should we understand the hadith cursing plucking eyebrows (nams)?

Yasir Qadhi • October 27, 2020 • Watch on YouTube
eyebrows plucking haram islamnamas haditheyebrows medical exception islamchanging creation allah

Quick Answer

The hadith on al-namisah (plucking eyebrows to change appearance) is authentic and severe in wording. Yasir Qadhi contrasts scholars who apply it literally without exception with others who ask for the underlying cause (changing Allah’s creation for vanity) and whether exceptions apply (e.g., medical need). He uses this as a case study in tolerating scholarly ijtihad differences.

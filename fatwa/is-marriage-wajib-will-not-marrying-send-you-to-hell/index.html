If someone never marries, will they go to Hell?

Yasir Qadhi • November 29, 2020 • Watch on YouTube
marriage obligatory islamcelibacy muslimnever marry islamibn taymiyah not marriedis marriage fard

Quick Answer

Marriage is encouraged when it is good for a person but is not wajib in the sense that celibacy by choice is not a sin. Notable scholars remained unmarried; attitudes may change with age. Not marrying does not take someone to Hell by itself.

Is it permissible for women to hold leadership roles and board positions in the masjid?

Yasir Qadhi • December 2, 2025 • Watch on YouTube
womenmasjidleadershipboardcommunity

Quick Answer

Nothing in the Sharia forbids a woman from being on a masjid board or holding leadership positions. Restricting women from permissible roles risks pushing them away from the community.

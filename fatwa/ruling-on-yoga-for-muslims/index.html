Is yoga permissible for Muslims?

Yasir Qadhi • December 19, 2025 • Watch on YouTube
yogaHinduismexercisecontext-based fatwapagan origins

Quick Answer

Yoga is permissible in the West where it is purely exercise with no religious connotation, but should be avoided by Muslims in India where it retains Hindu religious associations. This is context-dependent.

Does saying triple talaq in one sitting count as three divorces or one?

Yasir Qadhi • September 6, 2025 • Watch on YouTube
triple talaqtalaqdivorce in Islamthree talaq at onceIbn Taymiyyah triple talaq

Quick Answer

The correct opinion, which Yasir Qadhi personally holds, is that a triple talaq given in one sitting constitutes only one talaq — not three. This was the position of Ibn Taymiyyah, who was famously jailed for this fatwa. While all scholars agree that giving triple talaq at once is sinful and against the Sunnah, the majority of classical scholars held that it counts as three (making the divorce irrevocable). However, many modern fiqh councils have adopted Ibn Taymiyyah's position because of the practical hardship caused when husbands pronounce triple talaq in anger.

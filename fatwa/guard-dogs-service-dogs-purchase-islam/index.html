Is it allowed to own a guard dog or service dog, and may one purchase a dog?

Yasir Qadhi • October 20, 2020 • Watch on YouTube
dog guard house islamguide dog islambuying dog hadithservice dog muslimdog saliva najis

Quick Answer

Keeping dogs without need is strongly discouraged and affects rewards; dog saliva is najasah. The Qur'an allows hunting dogs; the Sunnah allows dogs for agriculture and shepherding. Many scholars extend this to any legitimate need—including guarding property or a medically needed assistance dog. Purchasing a dog: classical debate on the prohibition hadith; practical acquisition (adoption, nominal fee) is often discussed when purchase is problematic.

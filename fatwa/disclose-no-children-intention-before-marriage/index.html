Must you inform your future spouse if you do not want children?

Yasir Qadhi • October 20, 2020 • Watch on YouTube
no children marriage islamchildfree muslimdeception marriage islambarren marriage disclose

Quick Answer

If infertility is medical fact, concealing it from a prospective spouse is deceit and wrong. If one is fertile but strongly prefers no children, one should not hide that from the fiancé(e); marriage aims commonly include children, and mutual expectations should be clear—while remaining humble that feelings can change over time.

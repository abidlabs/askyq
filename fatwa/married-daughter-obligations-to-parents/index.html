How does a married daughter balance obligations between her husband and her parents?

Yasir Qadhi • December 2, 2025 • Watch on YouTube
parentsdaughterhusbandmarriageobligations

Quick Answer

A married woman's primary obligation shifts to her husband, unlike a son whose priority remains his mother. However, her relationship with parents never ceases. The husband is obligated to be fair and give her reasonable opportunity to care for her parents.

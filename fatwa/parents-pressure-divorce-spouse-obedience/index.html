Must a husband obey parents who pressure him to divorce his wife?

Yasir Qadhi • October 20, 2020 • Watch on YouTube
parents force divorce islamobey parents divorce wifein laws divorce pressureibn umar divorce hadith

Quick Answer

Obedience to parents is wide but not absolute when they command what harms the spouse without legitimate benefit, or what entails sin. After marriage, the wife has rights that must be balanced with parental rights; parents should not unreasonably demand divorce merely because they dislike the spouse. Ibn Taymiyyah’s principle: obey parents when their request benefits them without undue harm to the child—otherwise it is not obligatory.
